VEGETABLE SALAD

BENEFITS OF VEGETABLE

It works great for the persons with diabetes and Vegetable salads keep weight down. Salads contains a lot of fiber which ultimately helps in improving digestive health.

STEPS TO MAKE VEGETABLE SALAD

  • Put all your mixed greens and spinach in a large bowl.
  • Add in the cucumbers, carrots, bell pepper and tomatoes.
  • Put in the protein of your choice.
  • Top with the tasty toppings, which are your pumpkin seeds, basil leaves and chickpeas.
  • In a separate bowl, place all dressing ingredients and whisk until combined.
  • Toss all salad ingredients in a medium bowl.
  • Dress and plate your salad.
Leafy vegetable salad are a good choice for a healthful diet because they do not contain cholesterol and are naturally low in calories and sodium.
